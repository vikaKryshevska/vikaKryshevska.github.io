I've never done things with the umbrella

Umbrellas made primarily from bamboo tree.

Bamboo is renowned for its exceptional growth rate, often referred to as the "fastest-growing plant." It can reach full maturity in just a few years, making it an incredibly renewable and sustainable resource. By using bamboo in our umbrellas, we minimize the strain on natural resources and reduce deforestation. Bamboo has an impressive capacity to absorb carbon dioxide (CO2) from the atmosphere and release oxygen (O2) during photosynthesis. This makes bamboo forests effective carbon sinks, helping to mitigate climate change. By choosing umbrellas made from bamboo, you actively contribute to the fight against global warming.
